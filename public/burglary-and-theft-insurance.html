What is Burglary and Theft Insurance?

Burglary and theft insurance policy is a type of insurance policy that provides coverage for any financial losses or property damage led by an event of theft or burglary. The policy provides coverage in the event of unlawful entry or theft of personal assets to recover the financial loss incurred due to damage to assets and also recovery and replacement of stolen or damaged belongings.

What is Burglary

Burglary is a type of criminal activity which involves unlawful breaking or forceful entry within the property premises with the intention to commit robbery, vandalism or assault. Typically, this event of breaking and entering the property when the property owner is away, and cause severe damage to the property and loss of precious assets. Burglary could be residential, commercial or vehicle-based with specific consequences for each.

What is theft?

Theft is an unlawful activity which involves taking possession and control of another individual’s assets or property without permission irrespective of whether the entry was forceful or not. This comprises of acts such as shoplifting, stealing from vehicles, or pickpocketing which may consist of breaking into a structure.

Why do you need Burglary and Theft Insurance?

Burglary and theft insurance is crucial as it offers a safety net to shield the financial losses incurred during any event of burglary or theft. The policy coverage ensures protection against significant financial losses due to any event of theft or burglary. The insurance policy is a silent guardian that protects the property contents and helps recover from damages.

  • For Manufacturing Units: The burglary insurance covers machinery, raw materials, and finished goods, protecting the production capabilities from any event of theft and vandalism.

  • Retail Stores: Protects inventory, cash registers, and display items from shoplifting, burglary, and armed robbery.

  • Banks and Financial Institutions: Comprehensive burglary insurance covers cash, securities, and other valuable assets, providing robust protection against armed robbery and breaking and entering.

  • Warehouses: Coverage for high-value goods stored in warehouses, protecting against significant financial losses due to theft.

The burglary and theft insurance policy adds to the peace of mind for business owners and homeowners knowing the valuable assets are protected and in the event of theft or burglary, the financial burden to compensate damaged or stolen property contents and recovery will be covered.

What’s included in Burglary and Theft Insurance Coverage?

  • Loss or damage to personal property due to theft or burglary: Burglary insurance and theft insurance includes coverage for property contents like inventory, equipment and machinery with an add-on cover for personal items like electronic gadgets, jewellery, artwork and artefacts, and other precious possessions varying according to the insurer and insurance policy.

  • Damage caused to the property: In the event of burglary or theft, the insurance policy will cover the repair or replacement costs for damaged furniture, including doors, windows, and other structures on the insured property.

  • Add-on expenses: Due to the damages and effect of violent or forceful entry, if the property is not fit for business operations or residential utility, the burglary and theft insurance can provide for a temporary accommodation until the business operations can resume normally or the residential repairs are completed.

  • Liability coverage: Some burglary insurance and theft insurance policies may have liability coverage which can cover the legal expenses in the event of being sued due to break-in or robbery.

What’s not Covered? under Burglary and Theft Insurance?

  • Loss or damage due to war, riots or terrorism: The policy does not have coverage for loss or damage caused to the property due to any activity related to war or terrorism.

  • Loss or damage caused by intentional acts: When the theft or break-in or the damage caused to the property is intentional, the burglary and theft insurance policy will not provide the expense for repairs or replacement.

  • Loss or damage led by policyholder’s criminal activity: When the policyholder has planned the theft or burglary, the insurance company will not pay for the damages.

  • Loss or damage to items that are not listed in the policy: In several theft insurance and fire insurance policies, there is a limit for coverage amount for the personal precious assets and may not provide coverage for items which are beyond the limit and for the items which were not mentioned in the records during the inception of the policy.

  • Loss or damage caused by wear and tear: For any normal wear and tear to the property and the personal assets, the burglary and theft insurance will not provide any recovery or repair expenses.

How does Burglary and Theft Insurance work?

The burglary and theft insurance provides coverage for the damage caused to the property and personal assets within the property premises in the event of violent or unlawful entry with the intent of theft, vandalism or other criminal intent. When burglary and/or robbery occurs at the property, the policyholder (owner of the property) has to update the insurance company as well as report to the police authorities and follow their guidelines along with filing an FIR. The policyholder must file a claim and submit the required documents which includes property details, policy details and evidence of damage, FIR and other required details). The insurance company initiates the investigation by appointing a surveyor to estimate the total damage and tally it with policyholder’s statements and FIR. When all the details about the damages caused are clear and the claim assessment is complete, the insurance company compensates for the damages as per the conditions and coverage limits of the policy.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Burglary Insurance?

Burglary insurance helps protect the damages caused to the commercial or residential property in the event of an unlawful, forced or violent entry with the intention of theft, vandalism, or assault and cause significant damage to the property structure and contents which includes personal assets and precious valuables. Burglary insurance ensures financial protection for stolen and damaged property contents and structural damage due to such events.

What are the types of Burglary Insurance?

Burglary insurance can have a number of categories depending on the required coverage. It can be for residential or commercial (business) utility. Going further, it can also be categorized according to the extent of coverage needed and losses incurred.

First loss insurance: It is for theft of specific portion of property contents and the compensation is only for losses that exceed the first-loss level.

Full value policy: Coverage for complete value of insured assets and policyholders can claim the whole amount.

Stock declaration policy: For businesses who cannot precisely estimate the value of their stock, this policy provides coverage with the highest possible value of the inventory or property items to ensure adequate coverage for the evaluated loss.

What is the difference between Burglary Insurance and Theft Insurance?

The key difference between burglary insurance and theft insurance underlies in the scope of coverage. Burglary insurance provides coverage for any losses or damage of property contents only when there is unlawful, forced or violent entry into the property. This could also include criminal activities like vandalism or assault alongside theft. On the other hand, theft insurance provides coverage for theft of property contents without any forced, unlawful or violent entry.
