What is Product Liability Insurance ?

Product Liability Insurance safeguards companies from any kind of financial losses in the event that a sold product invokes harm to a consumer or causes property damage. This insurance provides coverage for the expenses related to legal defense along with after-verdict settlement costs as declared.

Why should you buy Product Liability ?

Product liability insurance is essential for businesses to protect from any financial losses due to any damage or harm by the company’s products sold to the consumers. This is a smart decision to insure not just the business but also the product liability insurance coverage shields the consumers who utilize the products. This includes legal expenses, damage recovery costs, and product recall expenses.

What’s included in Product Liability Insurance Coverage ?

  • Bodily Injury to Third Parties: The product insurance covers any kind of physical injury caused by using the company’s products.

  • Property Damage to Third-Party Property: For any claims about damage caused to the third party’s property, the product liability insurance will cover it.

  • Legal Defense Costs: In the event of a lawsuit filed by the consumer alleging damages invoked by the product, the product liability insurance coverage will pay the legal fees for defense and also cover any potential settlement costs after the verdict.

  • Product Recall Expenses: The policy provides coverage for the expenses related to recalling a faulty product.

  • Design Flaws: For any claims due to a faulty design related to the product, the product insurance will cover the expenses.

  • Manufacturing Defects: The policy will offer any costs incurred to manage the claims arising due to errors in the manufacturing process.

  • Warning/Labeling Defects: For any claims as a result of inefficient warnings or labels, the product liability insurance will provide the required recovery cover.

What’s not Covered in Product Liability Insurance Coverage ?

  • Intentional Acts: The policy does not offer coverage for intentional wrongdoing or deliberate damage.

  • Known Defects Prior To Policy Inception: The product insurance policy does not cover any validated pre-existing defects prominent before the company activated the policy.

  • Criminal Acts: There is no coverage for any illegal activities conducted while selling the products to the consumer or anything related to the process.

  • Contractual Liabilities: The product insurance policy does not provide any coverage in case of breach of contract.

  • Intellectual Property Infringement: The policy excludes coverage for any cases related to copyright infringement or patent ownership disputes.

  • Product Efficacy: The product liability does not cover any cases with failure to perform as advertised.

  • Pure Financial Loss: Any financial losses to the business which are beyond physical damage are not included in the product liability insurance coverage.

How does Property Insurance work?

The Product Liability Insurance protects from businesses from any kind of legal claims arising due o injuries or damages to third party caused by the company’s products. This can have manufacturing defects, flaws in the design, or inefficient warnings and guidelines related to the usage of the product. So, when a customer files a claim due to the harm caused by a product utility, the product liability policy supports the company in this event considering the exclusions and helps fight the lawsuit. In the event, the verdict issues payment to the customer, the policy covers the same shielding the company from further financial losses.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who takes product liability insurance?

The product liability insurance is vital for all kinds of business dealing with physical products which are into manufacturing, distributing and selling products. Any individual or company involved in the product supply chain can have a claim issued regarding the damage and injury caused to the consumers by the products, where the product insurance can help with financial protection.

What does Product Liability means?

Product liability is a law-related concept holding the manufacturers, retailers, sellers and/or any other party involved in the supply chain responsible in the event of the product being defective or for any damage or harm caused by the product to the customers or third party. The defects of the product could be a manufacturing, design flaw or any understated warning.

How much does Product Liability Insurance Cost?

The cost of a product liability insurance for a company can be influenced by a number of parameters which includes the industry type, the size of the company, product scale and range of the company, nature and type of product, the previous history of claims and the desired coverage limit.
