What is an Employee Wellness Plan?

Employee wellness plans (also known as employee benefits or group health coverage) are policies issued by companies to provide health coverage and wellness benefits for their employees. Depending on the plan chosen, these benefits may also extend to their immediate family members.

Who is Employee Wellness Plan for?

Employee wellness plans are designed for businesses of all sizes and industries. From small businesses and SMEs to startups and enterprises, these plans are applicable to all. In India, providing group health coverage to employees has become a mandatory requirement following the pandemic.

Why do you need a Employee Benefits Plan?

  • Improved Employee Satisfaction & Retention: Offering wellness programs reflects a strong commitment to employee well-being, and creates a sense of security for employees, helping to retain top talent.
  • Peace of Mind: Creates a positive work environment, promoting mental and physical well-being with a better work-life balance for employees.
  • Tax benefits: The premium for group health insurance can be used as business expenses under Section 17 of the Income Tax Act.
  • Cost-effective: With collective bargaining, the overall healthcare cost is reduced and businesses get comprehensive coverage at lower prices.
  • Securing best talent: The top talent finds wellness programmes attractive, making recruitment easier.

What’s Covered?

  • Pre-existing diseases : Expenses incurred for treatment of pre-existing diseases like diabetes are covered.
  • Pre & Post hospitalisation Expenses : Medical expenses incurred up to 30 days before and 60 days after hospitalisation are covered.
  • Ambulance : Expenses incurred for ambulance transportation to and from the hospital are covered.
  • Health Assistance Services : Dedicated medical care care service that assists you in all your health related queries.
  • Cashless Treatment : Avail cashless treatment at 6500 network hospitals pan India.
  • Room Rent : Room rent expenses incurred for hospitalizations are covered.
  • Maternity Benefits : Maternity care expenses for normal & C-section are covered in our premium plan.

What’s not Covered?

  • Infertility & Related Ailments : Expenses incurred for infertility & related ailments are not covered.
  • Admin/Service Expenses : Expenses incurred for fitting of prosthesis or devices contributing or replacing the function of an organ are not covered.
  • Non-medical Consumables : Items like syringes, surgical blades, crepe bandage, cotton, masks, gowns etc. which come under non- medical consumables are not covered under group health insurance policies.
  • OPD Expenses : OPD Expenses are not covered
  • Preventive Care : Expenses related to preventive care check-ups and related activities are not covered

What does a Employee Benefits Plan cost?

Several factors influence the cost of an employee benefits plan, such as the number of employees, the type of plan (e.g., whether dependents and parents are covered), the scope of benefits, prior claims history, and more.
